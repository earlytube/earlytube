EarlyTube is built by a parent, for parents. Children never interact with any sign-in, account, or data collection. The app is set up by a parent before a child uses it. This policy explains clearly what we collect and why.

Who uses EarlyTube

EarlyTube is a tool for parents and caregivers. It is not directed at children. The child-facing experience has no accounts, no forms, and no data collection of any kind. Only the parent interacts with sign-in and account features.

Google Sign-in & Firebase

If you choose to sign in, EarlyTube uses Google Sign-in via Firebase Authentication. When you sign in, we store the following in Firebase Firestore:

  • Your name and email address (from your Google account)
  • A link to your personal Google Sheet (created in your own Google Drive)

Sign-in is entirely optional. Firebase is a Google product. You can read Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Google Sheets & Drive

When you sign in and save videos, EarlyTube creates a Google Sheet in your own Google Drive in a folder called "EarlyTube". This sheet stores only the video titles and YouTube IDs you choose to save.

This data lives in your Google Drive. EarlyTube accesses it only to display and update your personal video list. We never share or sell it.

Parent PIN

The parent PIN you set is stored only on your device via your browser's local storage. It never leaves your device and is never sent to us. If you are signed in, it is also saved to your account in Firestore.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how people find and use EarlyTube — things like how many visitors we get and which pages are viewed. This data is anonymous and aggregated. We use it only to improve the app.

Google Analytics may use cookies and collect your IP address and browser information. Learn more at policies.google.com/privacy.

YouTube embeds

EarlyTube embeds videos from YouTube. When a video plays, YouTube's own privacy policy applies. YouTube may collect data about video views in accordance with Google's privacy policy. EarlyTube has no control over or access to that data.

Ko-fi support

EarlyTube is free. If you choose to support the project via Ko-fi, that transaction is handled entirely by Ko-fi and Stripe. We don't receive or store your payment details. See ko-fi.com/privacy.

Children's privacy (COPPA)

EarlyTube does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at earlytubeapp@gmail.com and we will delete it immediately.

Changes to this policy

If anything material changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. We will never add data collection quietly.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at earlytubeapp@gmail.com or via our contact page.
